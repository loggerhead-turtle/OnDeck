⚾ Built in: the walk-up flow on this page
  1. The deck fills this page with your batting order automatically (set it on the Lineup page) — one key per filled spot.
  2. Pressing a batter's key cues their walk-up — announcement + song load silently, ready to go.
  3. The always-on ▶ Play key (bottom left of the deck) runs the cued walk-up.
  4. When the song ends, the deck advances to the next spot by itself and cues that batter — spot 1, Play, spot 2, Play… wrapping back to the top. The current batter's key glows yellow.

Playing anything else (Hype, Celebrations, a one-off player) never advances the order. To change the batting order from the deck itself, add an Edit lineup (toggle) key plus Lineup spot keys: switch Edit Lineup on, tap a spot, then tap a player to fill it.
