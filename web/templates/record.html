🎙️ Record a song

Three ways to capture a song, cleanest first: upload a screen recording made with the phone's own audio (no microphone involved), capture a browser tab's audio on a computer, or record through the microphone while the song plays out loud.

📲 Best quality — use the phone's own audio

Phones can't share their internal audio with a web page directly, but their built-in screen recorder captures it perfectly. Record the screen while the song plays, then upload the video here — OnDeck keeps just the audio and throws the video away.

How to screen-record with sound

iPhone: Settings → Control Center → add “Screen Recording” once. Then swipe into Control Center, tap ⏺, play the song in your music app, stop, and the video lands in Photos.

Android 11+: Quick Settings → Screen record → set sound to “Device audio” (not microphone), play the song, stop.

Music-app audio is captured by the OS recorder even though the page can't hear it — that's the trick.
